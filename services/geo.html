What Is GEO

The next frontier
of organic visibility.

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) focuses on making your brand part of the long-form, context-rich answers that AI tools generate for complex queries - the kind your ideal buyers are asking daily.

⚡
GEO vs AEO

AEO targets short, direct answers (featured snippets and quick responses). GEO targets longer AI-generated content - research summaries, comparisons, and recommendations where your brand should be named as a credible option.

🧠
